Structural Testing & Standards

Compressive Strength Calibration

Every batch of concrete delivered to our project locations undergoes laboratory crush testing. We enforce a minimum grade of M40 for standard frames and M60 for high-load columns to ensure absolute safety.

Steel Tensile Grade Validation

All reinforcement bars undergo rigorous stress testing, guaranteeing ductility and elongation ratios that meet seismic load standards, shielding buildings from severe tectonic actions.

Eco-Carbon Compliance Logs

We keep strict ledger records of embodied carbon across our supply pipelines. By tracking sourcing indices, we target carbon neutrality across 85% of structural elements by 2030.
